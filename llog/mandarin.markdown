## Tough Characters ##

Because they look awfully alike to me, I tend to get them wrong quite often.

Characters|Mnemonic
:---------|:-------
方万|side vs 10,000
更便|more vs then
木体|wood vs body
立位|stand vs 
住往|live vs towards
间问|between vs ask
白日|white vs sun
半平|half vs flat
跟银|with vs silver
